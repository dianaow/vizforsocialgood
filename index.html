KIRON'S STUDENTS

Refugees coming to Germany are ambitious and are active. But they continue to have transition problems into the education system and the labour market. The main goal of Kiron is to provide free learning opportunities to refugees and asylum seekers, regardless of their education background, gender and age. The non-profit organization has developed an online study program to enable smooth access to higher education and short programmes with a closer labour market focus.
